Banana Carrot Papaya
Preparation: Slice the bananas evenly, about 1/4 to 3/8 inch thick.
Preparation: Slice the carrots into thin, uniform pieces, around 1/8 inch thick. Blanching them briefly in boiling water before dehydrating can help preserve color and flavor.
Preparation: Peel the papaya, remove the seeds, and cut into thin slices.
